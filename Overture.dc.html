Tainan Architectural Triennial

Overture

SiteTainan, Taiwan
Year2022
RoleExhibition design
Concept

In this exhibition, I am very honored to be the chairman of the students and lead all the students to hold this event. The exhibition was held in "THE SPRING", an international plaza designed by MVRDV. Almost the work of the whole year were exhibited, and I was fortunate to participate in all the projects from management, design, and execution. Feel very grateful to NCKU and Public Works Department for providing an opportunity for me to have this valuable practical experience.

The participants in this exhibition include the first-year, second- and fourth-year students of the architecture department of NCKU, and the M.Arch II students at the research institute. The curatorial plan is led by the second-year students (113 grade) to form a team and make the main design. Regardless of the covid-19 and the online stuff, we still plan to exhibit in a physical form, hoping to store images, ideals, and concepts in our minds through the structure of real existence.

The preparation for the exhibition was intensively discussed during the curatorial period from the end of April to mid-June and experienced more than ten meetings with classmates and teachers. In the early stage of preparation, the person in charge communicated with the teachers and students and discussed with the team members the details of the exhibition frame design, exhibition moving line, and main visual posters. During the process, meetings were held to modify the drawings and construction methods, and teachers and senior sisters were asked to assist and improve. In the later period, they participated in the physical exhibition installation process with other grades, and finally, the exhibition was successfully completed together.

Under the tight schedule, it is not easy for us to take care of the planning and preparation work for the first physical exhibition. In addition to taking design classes and exams into consideration, the students also use their spare time to continuously try out, discuss and adjust the finalization. This curation is undoubtedly a practical opportunity to test students' abilities, give full play to their strengths, and learn to cooperate with students across grades.

Thanks to many teachers, senior sisters, juniors, and sophomores for their active participation. I hope that in the life of co-existing with the epidemic, we can seize the opportunity to present the best physical exhibition effect to the citizens in an international exhibition venue.
