Mixed-Use Collective Housing

Hybrid

Siteadd site
Year2023
RoleIndividual studio project
Concept

This design concept originates from deconstructing the individual spaces of a collective housing complex. By modularizing its various functional spaces, living units of differing sizes can be assembled to suit the number and profile of the occupants. At the same time, when the different modular units are combined, the connecting zones in between are reserved as communal space, offering residents flexible options for shared facilities. Beyond this, the complex faces Tainan Park and sits at the heart of a green-belt core; accordingly, in addition to setting aside communal areas, we bring greenery into the interior, strengthening the interaction between nature and the residents.

設計 Design: 李思誼 / 楊品葳
