Urban Research

Railway Underground

SiteTainan Railway Corridor
Year2023
RoleResearch & design study
Concept

The site is Fu-Xing Market, located within the area of the Tainan railway undergrounding project and situated to the southwest of the Dong-Men Roundabout. It is the first culinary district that tourists arrive in Tainan by train encounter. The design concept transforms the market into an open public space: the ground floor houses a fresh-food market and food stalls, while the upper floors provide space for community residents' activities, creating spatial functions that meet the needs of both residents and visiting tourists. In coordination with the ground-level landscape redesign that follows Tainan's railway undergrounding, the north–south railway green-belt park at ground level extends its greenery into the market, injecting a new sense of public space and innovative possibilities into the ancient capital of Tainan.
